Venezuelan Recipe of the Arepa

Description

This is the true recipe to a venezuelan arepa.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups of Corn flour
  • 2 cups of Water
  • salt
  • 100grs of braised brisket
  • 20grs of grated yellow cheese

Steps

  1. Add the water into a bowl
  2. Add a pinch of salt into the water
  3. Add the flour into the water
  4. Mix it with your clean hands until a dough is made
  5. make discs forms with pieces of dough and place them into a pan until they're toasted
  6. fill it with some briket and yellow cheese and enjoy!
