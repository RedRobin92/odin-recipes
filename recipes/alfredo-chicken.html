Alfredo Chicken Recipe

Description

This is one of my girlfriend's favourite recipe, so now I bring it to you

Ingredients

  • 300 grs of chicken breasts cutted to strips
  • 2 minced garlic cloves
  • 1/2 cup of minced onion
  • 1tbsp butter
  • 1 cup of heavy cream

Steps

  • Heat a pan, and Cook the chicken strips into the butter until golden
  • Add the onion and the garlic into the pan
  • Once the onion is clrafied, add the heavy cream, lower the fire and when it boiles, leave it cook up to three minutes
  • You can eat it with white rice or spaghetti
