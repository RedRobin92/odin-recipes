Caesar Salad Dressing from a Scratch

One of the most popular salad recipes ever. Did you know it is not italian but its origin is mexican? Also, one of my favourites recipes of all time.

Ingredients>

  • 2 anchovy filets
  • 2 cloves of garlic
  • 1 cup mayonnaise
  • parmesan chees to taste
  • 2tbsp lemon juice
  • 2tsp worcerstershire sauce

Steps

  1. Make a paste with the anchovies and the farlic into a food processor
  2. then add the rest of the ingredients into the food processor and mix it
  3. Refrigerate at least one hour before serving
